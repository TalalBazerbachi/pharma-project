KULAX Syrup

 

§ Treatment of Constipation or when a soft stool is required such in case of: anal or colon surgery & hemorrhoids.

KULAX is an osmotic laxative (non absorbable disaccharide) broken down by colonic bacteria mainly into lactic acid & other acids, this exerts a local osmotic effect in the colon resulting in drawing fluid from body into the bowel, increased faecal bulk then stimulation of peristalsis (intestinal movements) & bowel evacuation.

 

§ For Hepatic Encephalopathy (portal systemic encephalopathy)

KULAX lowers the pH of the colon content which is important to convert ammonia to ammonium which will not be absorbed again then excreted with faeces. Also it kills the ammonia-producing organisms which are causing encephalopathy


Contraindications

KULAX should not be taken if the patient suffers from:galactosaemia or intestinal obstruction.

Precautions

KULAX is taken with caution in cases of Lactulose intolerance, pregnancy & breast-feeding.

Drug Interactions

Concomitant administration of KULAX with neomycin, antacids or other laxatives may decrease its effect.

Over-dosage

No cases of intoxication of Lactulose were reported, acute over dosage results in diarrhoea & abdominal cramps as major symptoms of over dosage.

 


Lactulose may cause abdominal discomfort associated with flatulence or cramps. Nausea and vomiting have occasionally been reported after high doses. Some consider the taste to be unpleasant (this can be minimised by dilution in water, fruit juice, or milk or by mixing the dose with food). Prolonged use or excessive dosage may result in diarrhoea with excessive loss of water and electrolytes, particularly potassium. Hypernatraemia has been reported rarely.

شراب كولاكس

 

§   لعلاج الإمساك كما في حالات جراحة القولون و البواسير:

يعتبر كولاكس ملين إسموزي (سكر ثنائي غير قابل للامتصاص) يتم تكسيره بواسطة بكتيريا القولون إلى أحماض عديدة أهمها حمض اللاكتيك و الذي له تأثير إسموزي ينتج عنه سحب الماء من الأنسجة إلى الأمعاء مما يزيد من كتلة البراز و يحفز حركة الأمعاء و من ثم تفريغ محتوى القولون.

 

§   لعلاج إعتلال الدماغ الكبدي

كولاكس يقلل درجة الحموضة لمحتويات القولون و هذا ضروري لتحويل الأمونيا إلى الأمونيوم غير القابلة للامتصاص و من ثم طردها مع البراز، كما أن الحموضة تعمل على قتل البكتيريا المنتجة للأمونيا و هذا يؤدي إلى تخليص الدم من الأمونيا المسببة لاعتلال الدماغ.

موانع الاستعمال

يمنع تناول كولاكس للمرضى الذين يعانون من انسداد الأمعاء و الجالاكتوزيميا (ارتفاع الجالاكتوز في الدم).

التحذيرات

كولاكس يؤخذ بحذر في حالات الحساسية المفرطة لللاكتيولوز و حالات الحمل و الإرضاع.

Mode of Action :

ATC code: A06AD11

In the colon lactulose is broken down by colonic bacteria into low molecular organic acids. These acids lead to a lowering of pH in the colonic lumen and via an osmotic effect to an increase of the volume of colonic contents. These effects stimulate peristalsis of the colon and return the consistency of the stool. The constipation is cleared and the physiological rhythm of the colon is reinstated.

In hepatic encephalopathy (HE) the effect has been attributed to suppression of proteolytic bacteria by an increase of acidophilic bacteria (e.g. lactobacillus), trapping of ammonia in the ionic form by acidification of the colonic contents, catharsis due to the low pH in the colon as well as an osmotic effect, and alteration of the bacterial nitrogen metabolism by stimulating the bacteria to utilize ammonia for bacterial protein synthesis.


Lactulose passes unchanged into the large intestine where it is metabolised by colonic bacteria with the formation of simple organic acids, mainly lactic acid and small amounts of acetic and formic acids, the diarrhoreal effect occurs in 48 hours post administration. The small amount of absorbed Lactulose is subsequently excreted unchanged in the urine.
