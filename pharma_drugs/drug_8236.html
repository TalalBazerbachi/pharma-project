BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief is a product which contains Ectoin®, a natural
cell-protective molecule with inflammation-reducing and membrane-stabilizing properties.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief soothes dry skin inside the ear canal and
alleviates pruritus caused by inflammatory dermatosis or allergies. It supports the regeneration
process of dry ear canal skin.
If used regularly, BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief helps to reduce the
incidence of otitis externa. In case of non-infectious otitis externa, it also reduces inflammation.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief cleans the ear canal. It supports
the hygiene of the ear canal after wearing hearing aids, in-ear headphones or ear plugs.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief helps against formation of ear wax plugs, if
used regularly
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief is a slightly hypertonic solution and supports
the softening of earwax in a natural way.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief is free of preservatives. The product contains
100% natural ingredients.


• BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief should not be used in ears with
damaged/perforated eardrums, inserted grommets (ventilation tubes), open wounds as e.g.
after surgery, or if the ear canal is plugged with foreign objects.
• If you suffer from otitis media or ear infection, BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal
Relief may only be used if recommended by your doctor/health care professional.
• Do not apply cold solution into the ear as it can cause discomfort and dizziness.
• BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief should not be used in the case of
hypersensitivity to Ectoin® or any of the other ingredients of the ear spray.
• If allergic reactions occur, the use of BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief must
be discontinued immediately.
• To ensure safe hygiene, each spray bottle should be used by one person only.
• Do not use for more than 6 weeks after opening.

There are no known interactions between BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief and
other ear sprays. Wait at least 15 minutes after using BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal
Relief before applying any other product or drug into the ear.
The ear canal should always be dry, before you insert hearing aid, in-ear headphones or ear
plugs.

 


Always use BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief according to these instructions for
use. Consult your healthcare provider or pharmacist before use, if you are not sure.
If not otherwise prescribed by your doctor, please apply 1-2 sprays into the affected ear several
times a day.
Children 10 years of age and younger should be assisted during the application of BETADINE®
SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief.
Please apply BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief after wearing hearing aids,
in-ear headphones, or ear plugs, to allow excess earwax and debris to drain out the ear canal in a natural way.
Before using BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief for the first time, pump 2-3
times to release the air from the pump. If the spray function is impaired (which might happen if
the bottle is not positioned upright), press the pump mechanism several times in upright
position.
1. Only if the bottle feels cold, warm it up by holding it in your hand.
2. Hold the bottle upright in your hand and place your index finger on top of the spray head.
3. Spray the solution into your affected ear.
4. Tilt your head to the side to let run out the excess liquid and dry the ear pinna, if necessary.
5. Wipe the exposed tip of the bottle with a clean lint-free cloth.


BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief is well tolerated. Up to now, no regularly
occurring or persisting side effects are known.
Please inform your doctor or pharmacist if you notice side effects that are associated with the
use of BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief.
If conditions persist or worsen (e.g. strong pain) or symptoms of an infection (as e.g. ear
discharge) appear, contact your doctor or pharmacist.


The expiry date of BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief is printed on the box and
on the bottle. BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief must not be used after the
expiry date has passed.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief should be stored below 25 °C. Do not freeze
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Ear Canal Relief should be kept out of reach of children.
