BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is an isotonic solution which contains Ectoin®, a
natural cell-protective molecule with inflammation-reducing as well as membrane- and
lipid-stabilizing properties.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is able to reduce inflammations caused by an
irritated or dry conjunctiva which are major problems of the dry eye syndrome.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes alleviates symptoms of dry eyes such as burning,
dryness, itching.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes moistens dry eyes and additionally prevents from
further dehydration.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes stabilizes the lipid layer of the tear film and
protects ocular cells from hyperosmolarity.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes supports the regeneration process of dry and
irritated conjunctiva.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is suitable for sensitive eyes.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is free of preservatives.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is suitable for preventive use.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is suitable for contact lens wearers.


• If recommended by your doctor/health care professional, BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry &
Tired eyes may be used after eye/ocular surgery and trauma.
• BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes should not be used in the case of
hypersensitivity to Ectoin® or any of the other ingredients of the eye drops.
• If allergic reactions occur, the use of BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes must be
discontinued immediately.
• Do not use damaged or punctured ampoules.
• After usage of one ampoule, the remaining liquid must be discarded; it is not intended for later
usage.

 

There are no known interactions between BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes and
other eye drops or topical eye ointments.
Wait at least 15 minutes after using BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes before
applying any other product or drug into the eye.


Always use BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes according to these instructions for use.
Consult your healthcare provider or pharmacist before use if you are not sure.
If not otherwise prescribed by your healthcare provider, please apply 1-2 drops per eye, several times
a day. Children 10 years of age and younger should be assisted during the application of BETADINE®
SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes
1. Detach one ampoule from the strip and open the unit by twisting off the tab.
2. Bend your head backwards, with your forefinger pull the lower eyelid downwards and look up.
3. Gently squeeze the ampoule together using the other hand and let one to two drops fall into thepocket of the eye.
4. The ampoule should not touch the eye. Close your eye slowly to disperse the solution
throughout the eye as good as possible.
5. Repeat the procedure on the other eye.


BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is well tolerated. Up to now, no regularly occurring
or persisting side effects are known.
In isolated cases, BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes may provoke short term eye
irritation, ocular hyperaemia, or conjunctivitis.
Please inform your healthcare provider, pharmacist.
Representative of the product if you notice side effects that are associated with the use of
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes. If conditions persist or worsen, contact your
healthcare provider or pharmacist


The expiry date of BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is printed on the box and on the
ampoule.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes must not be used after the expiry date has passed.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes should be stored below 25 °C. Do not Freeze.
BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry & Tired eyes is filled in small plastic parts that could be
swallowed by small children. Danger of suffocation. Therefore, BETADINE® SOOTHING RELIEF Dry &
Tired eyes should be kept out of reach of children.


Ingredients
Ectoin®, Hydroxyethyl Cellulose, Sorbitol, Water, and Citrate Buffer
