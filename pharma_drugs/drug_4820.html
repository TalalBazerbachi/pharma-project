This medicine contains

(Simethicone which is an anti-flatulent (helps reduce trapped wind

These tablets are for the relief of symptoms associated with passing wind, wind pains, bloating and trapped wind


Do not take this medicine if you

o   are allergic to Simethicone or any of the other ingredients

Please see your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you

o   have an intolerance to some sugars

o   are taking levothyroxine for thyroid disorders

Pregnancy and breast feeding

If you are pregnant, see your doctor or pharmacist for advice before taking these tablets. They may be taken if you are breastfeeding

Information about some of the ingredients in this medicine

Contains sorbitol. If you have been told by your doctor that you have an intolerance to some sugars, contact your doctor before taking this medicinal product

Other medicines and FLATICON Chewable Tablets

If you are being treated with levothyroxine for a thyroid disorder, using Flaticon might reduce the amount of this type of medicine that is absorbed and weaken the effect of your treatment


Always take this medicine exactly as your doctor or pharmacist has told you. Check with your doctor or pharmacist if you are not sure. Take this medicine by mouth

Chew the tablet before swallowing. To be taken after meals or at bedtime

Adults, the elderly and children over 12 years

2-3 tablet, 3 or 4 times a day or as required. Do not take more than 500mg in any 24 hour period

Do not give to children under 12 years of age

Do not exceed the stated dose

If your symptoms persist or worsen, you must see a doctor or pharmacist

If you take too many tablets by mistake, contact your doctor or pharmacist straight away

If you have any further questions on the use of this medicine, ask your doctor or pharmacist


Like all medicines this medicine can cause side effects, although not everybody will get them

If any of the side effects gets serious, or if you notice any side effects not listed in this leaflet, please tell your doctor or pharmacist

If you experience any of the following stop using the medicine and seek immediate medical help

allergic reactions such as rash, itchiness, swelling of the face or tongue and breathing difficulties

If you experience any of the following stop taking the medicine and talk to your doctor

o   stomach upset and diarrhea

If you get any side effects, talk to your doctor, pharmacist. This includes any possible side effects not listed in this leaflet


o   Keep out of the reach and sight of children

o   Store below 30°C

o   Keep in the original pack to protect from light

o   Do not take FLATICON after the expiry date printed on the pack. The expiry date refers to the last day of that month

o   Do not throw any medicines via wastewater or household waste. Ask your pharmacist how to throw away medicines you no longer use. These measures will help protect the environment


The active substance is Simethicone

Tablets: Each tablet contains Simethicone Powder eq. to Simethicone 40 mg or 60 mg

Other ingredients: Calcium Phosphate, Maize Starch (1500 ) , Saccharin Sodium, Mannitol, Croscarmellose Sodium, Sorbitol, Povidone (K30), Peppermint Flavour P96511-71 and Magnesium Stearate

Flaticon Chewable Tablets

Each chewable tablet contains simethicone equivalent to simethicone 40 mg or 60 mg. For excipients, see 6.1

Chewable tablet Flaticon 60mg : White to off white , circular , flat , with beveled edges, uncoated chewable tablets with embossing inscription “mc70” on one side and a break line on the other side with mint odour Flaticon 40mg : White to off white , circular , biconvex , , uncoated chewable tablets with embossing inscription “mc69” on one side and a break line on the other side with mint odour

Antiflatulent defoaming agent for the symptomatic relief of flatulence, wind pains, bloating, abdominal distension and other similar symptoms associated with gastrointestinal gas


Adults, elderly and children over 12 years

2-3tablet to be taken three or four times daily or as required for relief, after meals and upon retiring. The tablets are to be chewed before swallowing


The product should not be used in patients with known hypersensitivity to any of the ingredients

If symptoms persist for more than 14 days, or if symptoms worsen, medical advice must be sought

Keep out of the reach and sight of children

Not to be taken by children under 12 years

This product contains lactose

Patients with rare heredity problems of galactose intolerance, the Lapp lactase deficiency or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine

This product contains sorbitol

Patients with rare heredity problems of fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption or sucraseisomaltose insufficiency should not take this medicine


Levothyroxine may bind to the silica in simethicone which can impair the absorption of levothyroxine


Medical advice should be sought prior to taking this product if pregnant


None known
