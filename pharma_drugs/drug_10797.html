Description
Hyalfid gel is an isotonic sterile ophthalmic solution with hyaluronic acid sodium salt 0.30% (FHA 1.0), and amino acids, preservative free, 

 Aimed at complementing tear secretion and restoring tear film physiology

Hyalfid gel protects and lubricates the surface of the eye, providing
relief from mechanical, environmental, and visual stress, or in the event of severe reduction in corneal-conjunctival lubrication. Hyaluronic
acid is a mucopolysaccharide physiologically present in the human eye and has the property of adhering to the surface of the eye
protecting and lubricating it.


This product is for external ophthalmic use only.
• To avoid damage to the surface of the eye, do not allow the dropper to come into contact with the eye during instillation.
• As the solution in the bottle runs out, more force will need to be applied to create a drop.
• Close the bottle tightly after use.
• If used alongside topical ophthalmic medicines, consult your doctor.
• Do not use the product if the OSD bottle is damaged.
• Keep the product out of the sight and reach of children.


 Apply 1-2 drops to the conjunctival sac 3 or more times a day.

The product may be instilled into the eyes even when wearing contact lenses. 

Instructions for use

When first used, remove the plastic ring that seals the cap by puling the side tab (1),

- Remove the cap (2).

Turn the device over the eye and apply increasing pressure on the bottle with your index finger and thumb until the drop (3)

comes out. As the solution in the bottle becomes depleted, more force may be required (4).

Close the bottle again with the cap (5)


No undesirable effects have been reported after administration of the product reactions,

In case of advarse reactions, stop using the product and consult a physician.

After application of the product ,there may  be a slight burning of the eye and temporary visual discomfort. 


Do not store above 25°C.

Do not use the product after the expiry date indicated on the bottle label 

After first opening, the product may be used until the expiry date indicated on the packaging

Do not discard in the environment.


Main component: Sodium Hyaluronate 0.30% (FHA 1.0)

Other components: Boric acid, Disodium tetraborate decahydrate, Sodium chloride,Glycine, L-leucine, L-Lysine hydrochloride, L-Proline, Water for injection.


Hyalfid gel bottle is fitted with an OSD (Ophthalmic Squeeze Dispenser) dropper which, through its multi-layer air filter and tip-seal valve system, ensures that the sterility of the product is maintai- ned without preservatives
