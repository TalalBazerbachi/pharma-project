RIBOUSIN monodose is an isotonic sterile ophthalmic solution with hyaluronic acid sodium salt (FHA 1.0), Vitamin B2 (Riboflavin Phosphate) and amino acids,

preservatives free, aimed at restoring tear secretion and tear film physiology.

RIBOLISIN monodose protects and lubricates the ocular surface, providing relief from disorders caused by photooxidative damage.
Hyaluronic acid is a mucopolysaccharide physiologically present in the human eye and has the property of adhering to the
ocular surface by protecting and lubricating it. Due to its mucoadhesive properties, it remains on the ocular surface for a
long time and stabilises the tear film.
Vitamin B2 offers protection against damage resulting from exposure to UV-A light, both from natural source and from
irradiation during surgical treatments (e.g. corneal cross-linking) as well as from damage due to blue light emitted by
screens and visual aids, halogen lamps, monitors and televisions.
The amino acid formulation helps lubricate the surface of the eye and improves the protection of epithelia subject to
various kinds of stress

 

RIBOUSIN monodose is indicated for hydration and protection of the ocular surface from photooxidative damage.


• This product is for external ophthalmic use only.
• To eliminate the risk of contamination of the solution, make sure the single-dose container does not come into contact
with the eye or other objects.
• To avoid damage to the ocular surface, do not allow the container to come into contact with the eye during instillation.
• The product is in single-dose packaging. After administration, the residual contents of the single-dose container should
be disposed of as the product contains no preservatives. Reuse of residual content may result in an ophthalmic infection
and/or damage to the patient.
• The product is in single-dose packaging. The volume contained in the single-dose can be used for a single dose.
• I f used alongside topical ophthalmic medicines, consult your doctor.
• Do not use the product if the single-dose container is damaged.
• Keep the product out of the sight and reach of children.


• Separate a single-dose container from the strip.
• Open it by twisting off the top.
• Apply 1 - 2 drops on the conjunctival sac.
• Discard the closed single-dose container with the remaining contents


No undesirable effects have been reported after administration of the product. In case of adverse reactions, stop using the
product and consult a physician. After application of the product, there may be a slight burning of the eye and temporary
visual discomfort.


Do not store above 25°C. Do not use the product after the expiry date printed on the single-dose container.
Do not use the product after six months from the opening of the aluminium sachet. Do not discard in the environment.


Main component: 0.15% Sodium Hyaluronate (FHA 1.0),
Other components: Vitamin B2 (Riboflavin Phosphate), boric acid, disodium tetraborate decahydrate, sodium chloride,
glycine, L-leucine, L-lysine hydrochloride, L-proline, water for injection.


15 single-dose containers (0.35 ml)
